Vellora

Designing a Seamless Bicycle Discovery & Purchase Experience

Project Overview

Vellora is a mobile commerce experience designed to simplify how users explore, customise, and purchase performance bicycles through an immersive and visually guided interface. The project focuses on improving product discoverability, decision clarity, and checkout flow efficiency in high-involvement purchase scenarios such as sports equipment buying.

Role: UI / UX Designer
Platform: Mobile
Scope: Product browsing · configuration · cart interaction · checkout flow
