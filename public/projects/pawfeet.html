PawFeet

Mobile App — Pet Adoption Platform

PawFeet is a thoughtfully designed mobile application that connects loving families with pets in need of a home. The platform simplifies the adoption journey by making it easy to discover, connect, and adopt dogs and other pets through a seamless digital experience.

Project Overview

Welcome to my first UX case study. In this case study, we'll look at designing a pet adoption application. I intend to complete this project using previously done course, so I follow the structure below. Which is Google Design Professional certification course that I completed a few years ago. Let's start.

Design prompt:
Pets which don't have a roof over their heads or food to eat need our help.

The Problem:
To get a pet, people generally go to pet stores, but they have to move a long distance and they have to take the animal without proper guidance. Available online platforms are clustery and poorly designed.

An Overview:
The application allows users to schedule a time to see their favorite pets. They talk on the phone with the pet to see if it looks the same. At the same time, the user can visit the local pet store and get their pet.
